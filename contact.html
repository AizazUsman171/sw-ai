Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about working with me and my AI development services

How long does a typical AI project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity. Simple automation workflows can be completed in 1-2 weeks, while complex AI systems like trading algorithms or recommendation engines typically take 1-3 months. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

What's included in the free consultation?

During our 30-minute consultation, we'll discuss your business needs, explore AI opportunities, review technical requirements, and outline a potential solution strategy. You'll receive actionable insights even if we don't proceed with a project.

Do you provide ongoing support after project completion?

Yes, I offer comprehensive post-launch support including bug fixes, performance optimization, feature updates, and training. Support packages can be customized based on your needs and budget.

What technologies do you specialize in?

I specialize in LangChain, OpenAI GPT-4, CrewAI, AutoGPT, Langgraph for AI development; N8N, Zapier, Make.com for automation; and Python, TensorFlow, PyTorch for machine learning. I also work with cloud platforms like AWS and Azure.

How do you ensure data security and privacy?

I follow strict security protocols including data encryption, secure APIs, compliance with GDPR/CCPA regulations, and signed NDAs. All client data is handled with the highest level of confidentiality and security.

Can you work with existing systems and integrations?

Absolutely! I have extensive experience integrating AI solutions with existing business systems, databases, CRMs, and third-party APIs. I'll ensure seamless integration without disrupting your current workflows.
